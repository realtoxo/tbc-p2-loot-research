# SSC/TK Raid-Night Tier Token Quick Sheet

Source basis:
- Full token plan: [SSC/TK Tier 5 Token Priority and Equip Constraints](ssc-tk-tier-token-priority.md)
- Broader LC guide: [SSC/TK Loot Priority Guide](ssc-tk-loot-priority-lc-guide.md)
- Primary video transcript: Zatar, `TBC Classic Phase 2 Loot Priority [Fixed]`, https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xaTf4e4y-mc

Use this during raid. The goal is not to re-litigate the whole loot sheet on Discord; it is to answer the three questions that matter when a token is on the corpse.

## Universal Three Questions

For every token, ask:

1. Does this complete or materially advance a protected set objective?
   - Highest priority examples: Mage 2p, Rogue 4p, Hunter 4p, Enhance 4p, main-tank/tank-set pieces, Holy Priest 4p, Boomkin/Shadow 4p when close.
2. Can the player equip it tonight without breaking something better?
   - Watch for Hunter Beast Lord 4p, Enhancement T4 2p, Mage Spellfire/T5 routing, Rogue Bloodsea chest route, Holy Priest Primal Mooncloth chest, tank crush/defense requirements, and engineering helm setups.
3. If it will be banked, is LC committing to finish that player soon?
   - If yes, award toward the planned set. If no, prefer a real immediate equip or the next protected set plan.

## Pre-Raid Setup

Have this written before pull:

| Track | Pre-raid note |
|---|---|
| Mages | Which Mages still need 2p? Which slots activate it fastest? |
| Hunters | Which Hunter is being stacked to 4p first? Are they BM with non-tier legs, or Survival with tier legs? |
| Rogues | Which Rogue is on helm/shoulders/gloves/legs plus `Bloodsea Brigand's Vest`? |
| Enhance | Which four pieces are planned, and will a token break T4 2p too early? |
| Tanks | Which tanks can equip tier without losing crush-immunity, defense, stamina, or required threat? |
| Priests/Druids | Which Defender users are close to 4p, and which are only asking for a single-slot upgrade? |
| Warlocks | Which offpieces are owed instead of tier: `Vestments of the Sea-Witch`, `Wand of the Forgotten Star`, `Fang of the Leviathan`, `Fathomstone`, boots, rings? |

## Leotheras: Gloves Tokens

### Vanquished Champion Gloves: Paladin, Rogue, Shaman

Default: Rogue > Prot Paladin > Enhance > biggest upgrade.

Ask:
1. Does this move a Rogue on the correct non-chest 4p path toward 4p?
2. If not, can a Prot Paladin equip these tonight without breaking tank requirements?
3. If Enhance wants them, are gloves part of a near-term 4p swap, or just a random single piece?

Do not:
- Give to Enhance if it causes an early, bad T4 2p break.
- Give to Ret/Holy/Resto while Rogue 4p or Prot Paladin gloves are unresolved, unless the token would otherwise rot.

### Vanquished Defender Gloves: Warrior, Priest, Druid

Default: Feral Cat/Bear > Holy Priest > Prot Warrior/Shadow/Boomkin by 4p proximity > Resto Druid > Fury.

Ask:
1. Is there a real Feral user, either Bear set value or Cat 4p path?
2. Does this help Holy Priest finish or advance the Primal Mooncloth chest 4p route?
3. Among Prot Warrior, Shadow, and Boomkin, who is closest to an actual 4p plan?

Do not:
- Give to Fury while real Defender set users still need them.
- Give isolated Shadow/Boomkin gloves unless LC intends to finish their 4p.

### Vanquished Hero Gloves: Hunter, Mage, Warlock

Default: Mage > Hunter > Warlock.

Ask:
1. Does this activate or move toward Mage 2p?
2. If Mages are covered, is this one of the four planned Hunter pieces for the Hunter being stacked?
3. If neither, is a Mage 4p project closer than any Hunter 4p project?

Do not:
- Give to Warlock except as uncontested filler.
- Give to a Hunter who will bank it with no LC plan to finish 4p.

## Fathom-Lord Karathress: Legs Tokens

### Vanquished Champion Legs: Paladin, Rogue, Shaman

Default: Prot Paladin, unless Rogue completes 4p that week > Elemental > Enhance if completing 4p > Holy Paladin > Resto Shaman > Ret.

Ask:
1. Does a Prot Paladin need legs and can they equip them safely tonight?
2. Does this complete Rogue 4p on the preferred helm/shoulders/gloves/legs route?
3. If neither, is this an Elemental immediate equip or an Enhance 4p completion piece?

Do not:
- Give to Rogue as a random single over Prot Paladin legs.
- Give to Ret unless all real Champion users are done.

### Vanquished Defender Legs: Warrior, Priest, Druid

Default: Bear > Holy Priest > Prot Warrior/Boomkin/Shadow by 4p proximity > Resto Druid > Fury.

Ask:
1. Does the main Bear need legs for a real tank set?
2. Does Holy Priest need them for the no-tier-chest 4p plan?
3. Which of Prot Warrior, Boomkin, or Shadow gets closest to 4p by taking them?

Do not:
- Auto-award to Boomkin if they are using `Spellstrike Pants` and legs are not in the plan.
- Give to Fury while Bear/Holy Priest/4p Defender projects are active.

### Vanquished Hero Legs: Hunter, Mage, Warlock

Default: Mage > Survival Hunter or Hunter with real planned use > Warlock.

Ask:
1. Does this activate or advance Mage 2p/4p?
2. Is the Hunter Survival, or otherwise using tier legs in their planned 4p?
3. If BM Hunter wants them, why are they not using `Leggings of Murderous Intent` as offset?

Do not:
- Give BM Hunter legs just because they are Hunter tier if their planned 4p excludes legs.
- Give to Warlock unless Mage/Hunter plans are covered and the Warlock will equip them.

## Lady Vashj: Helm Tokens

### Vanquished Champion Helm: Paladin, Rogue, Shaman

Default: Rogue > Enhance > Ret/Holy/Resto > Prot > Elemental.

Ask:
1. Does this advance or complete Rogue 4p?
2. Is Enhance close enough to 4p that helm creates the final or near-final swap?
3. If a healer/Ret/Prot wants it, will they equip it tonight and is Rogue/Enhance covered?

Do not:
- Give Prot Paladin helm before checking engineering goggles and tank stat requirements.
- Give Elemental helm while T4 helm is still the better route.

### Vanquished Defender Helm: Warrior, Priest, Druid

Default: Main Bear > Balance/Holy Priest > Prot Warrior/Shadow only if committing to 4p > Fury > Resto Druid.

Ask:
1. Is the main Bear missing helm for a tank set?
2. Does Balance or Holy Priest need helm for a clean 4p route?
3. If Prot Warrior or Shadow wants it, is helm actually part of the planned set, or is engineering/no-helm routing better?

Do not:
- Give Shadow Priest helm by default; the cleaner route often avoids helm.
- Give Fury helm before real Defender set users are covered.

### Vanquished Hero Helm: Hunter, Mage, Warlock

Default: Mage > Hunter > Warlock.

Ask:
1. Does this activate or advance Mage 2p/4p?
2. Does this advance the Hunter being stacked to 4p?
3. If Warlock wants helm, are all Mage 2p and Hunter 4p priorities handled?

Do not:
- Give to Hunter as an isolated bank piece unless LC is finishing that Hunter soon.
- Give to Warlock as compensation for missing offpieces; Warlock needs offpieces, not token charity.

## Void Reaver: Shoulders Tokens

### Vanquished Champion Shoulders: Paladin, Rogue, Shaman

Default: Rogue > Holy Paladin > Resto Shaman > Prot > others.

Ask:
1. Is a Rogue on the correct helm/shoulders/gloves/legs route?
2. If Rogue is covered, which healer will equip shoulders and gain the most real value?
3. Does Prot Paladin actually need shoulders, or are they lower than legs/gloves/helm alternatives?

Do not:
- Give to Enhance/Ret by default; their normal physical shoulder target is `Shoulderpads of the Stranger`.
- Force Rogue into tier chest later by skipping tier shoulders now.

### Vanquished Defender Shoulders: Warrior, Priest, Druid

Default: Bear > Prot Warrior > Balance/Holy Priest > Shadow if finishing 4p > Resto/Fury.

Ask:
1. Does Bear or Prot Warrior need these for a real tank set?
2. Does Balance or Holy Priest need them for 4p?
3. Does this finish Shadow 4p, or is it just another weak single piece?

Do not:
- Give to Fury except as leftover.
- Give to Resto Druid over active tank or 4p Defender plans unless it is a deliberate healer equity call.

### Vanquished Hero Shoulders: Hunter, Mage, Warlock

Default: Mage > Hunter > Warlock.

Ask:
1. Does this activate or advance Mage 2p/4p?
2. Is this one of the Hunter's planned 4p pieces?
3. If Warlock asks, why is this better than routing them to offpieces?

Do not:
- Give to Warlock except as uncontested filler.
- Spread Hero shoulders across Hunters if one Hunter is supposed to hit 4p first.

## Kael'thas: Chest Tokens

### Vanquished Champion Chest: Paladin, Rogue, Shaman

Default: Enhance > Resto/Elemental/Holy > Ret/Rogue/Prot.

Ask:
1. Does Enhance need chest for the standard 4p path?
2. If not, which Shaman/Paladin healer or Elemental Shaman will equip it immediately?
3. If Rogue wants it, why are they not using `Bloodsea Brigand's Vest` plus tier shoulders?

Do not:
- Give Rogue chest by default. It creates a bad shoulder/offpiece problem.
- Give Prot or Ret chest while Enhance or real caster/healer Champion chest users need it.

### Vanquished Defender Chest: Warrior, Priest, Druid

Default: Prot Warrior > Feral > Balance/Shadow working 4p > Resto Druid > Fury > Holy Priest.

Ask:
1. Does Prot Warrior need this exceptional chest for a real tank set?
2. If not, does Feral need it for Bear or Cat 4p routing?
3. Does Balance or Shadow need it to complete or materially advance 4p?

Do not:
- Give Holy Priest chest early; `Primal Mooncloth Robe` is the normal route.
- Give Fury chest while tank/Feral/caster 4p users still need it.

### Vanquished Hero Chest: Hunter, Mage, Warlock

Default: Mage > Hunter > Warlock.

Ask:
1. Does Mage need chest for 2p/4p routing while preserving Warlock's Vashj chest claim?
2. Is this one of the Hunter's planned 4p pieces, and will it be equipped with the batch?
3. If Warlock wants chest, is `Vestments of the Sea-Witch` unavailable long enough that tier chest is a real backup?

Do not:
- Give Warlock tier chest before protecting Warlock `Vestments of the Sea-Witch`.
- Give Hunter chest as an isolated bank piece if no 4p plan exists.

## Fast Tie-Breakers

Use these when the correct lane is clear but two players are inside that lane:

| Tie | Break by |
|---|---|
| Two Mages need 2p | Best attendance/performance first, then fastest 2p activation. |
| Two Rogues need 4p | Finish the Rogue already closest to 4p unless player reliability says otherwise. |
| Two Hunters need 4p | Stack one Hunter to 4p; do not split unless both can finish in the same lockout window. |
| Tank token dispute | Main tank role and actual equip set first; theoretical offspec sets second. |
| Healer token dispute | Reliability and assignment impact first, then compensate the healer who waits with mace/cloak/rings/tier later. |
| Banked set piece vs immediate equip | Banked piece wins only if LC is committing to finish that set soon. |
