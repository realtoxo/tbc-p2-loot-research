Use this when the LC question is not "who gets this boss drop?" but "where do this week's [Nether Vortexes](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30183/nether-vortex) and pattern drops create the most raid value?"

## Executive Rule

1. Do not spread early [Nether Vortexes](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30183/nether-vortex) across half-finished projects. Pick the first committed craft and finish it.
2. Finish committed blacksmith weapon upgrades first, especially [Dragonstrike](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=28439/dragonstrike) for a core Fury Warrior Hammersmith.
3. After the blacksmith weapon lane is handled, stockpile toward high-demand BoE belt crafts, especially [Belt of Blasting](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30038/belt-of-blasting).
4. Treat BoP craft recipes as player-recipient loot, not guild-crafter loot. The most common trap is awarding a boot pattern to the wrong profession character because they are the "guild tailor" or "guild leatherworker."

## Craft Ownership Matrix

| Craft type | LC handling | Main trap |
|---|---|---|
| Blacksmith weapon upgrades | Give Vortexes directly to the player who will craft and equip the weapon. | Splitting Vortexes across several players and leaving everyone unfinished. |
| BoE belt crafts | Give Vortexes to the reliable pattern holder/crafter, then assign the finished belt through normal LC. | Treating "who gets the pattern" as the same question as "who gets the first belt." Belts can be made for multiple people once the Vortexes exist. |
| BoP boot crafts | Award the recipe only to the player who can craft and equip the boots. | Giving a boot pattern to the guild crafter when that character cannot use the item. The transcript explicitly uses [Boots of the Long Road](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30035/boots-of-the-long-road) on a Warlock tailor as the failure case. |
| BoE pattern / BoP craft leatherworking boots | Let the intended wearer buy or receive the pattern if they have Leatherworking. | Forgetting that the finished item is personal even if the pattern itself can move. |

## Officer Checklist

Before assigning any [Nether Vortexes](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30183/nether-vortex) or pattern:

1. Is this a Vortex spend or a BoP recipe-recipient problem?
2. Is the craft BoE or BoP after it is made?
3. Who can craft it today, and who will actually equip it today?
4. Does this finish a committed blacksmith weapon or just start another partial project?
5. If this is a belt, is [Belt of Blasting](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30038/belt-of-blasting) still blocking six-plus caster upgrades?
6. If this is a boot recipe, are we about to hand it to the wrong profession character?
7. If this is a leatherworking craft, has LC checked whether the intended wearer actually has Leatherworking or is willing to keep it long enough to use the item?

## Common Bad Calls

| Bad call | Why it is bad |
|---|---|
| Giving the first two Vortexes to two different players | Neither craft gets finished. Funnel until the selected project is complete. |
| Awarding [Boots of Blasting](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30037/boots-of-blasting) or [Boots of the Long Road](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30035/boots-of-the-long-road) to the generic guild tailor | Boots are personal crafts; the pattern belongs on the wearer. |
| Spending early Vortexes on [Lionheart Executioner](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=28430/lionheart-executioner) before checking the Ret/Arms Twinblade plan | [Twinblade of the Phoenix](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=29993/twinblade-of-the-phoenix) can reduce the urgency of the two-handed blacksmith craft. |
| Making a narrow belt before the first [Belt of Blasting](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30038/belt-of-blasting) wave | [Belt of Blasting](https://www.wowhead.com/tbc/item=30038/belt-of-blasting) has the broadest caster demand and should be mass-produced once the weapon lane is handled. |
| Treating Enhancement blacksmithing as automatic | The Enhancement transcript evidence is cautious: slow weapon drops and race-specific paths matter. Require a named craft plan before spending Vortexes. |
| Treating a BoE belt pattern as if only one player benefits | Once the crafter has the pattern and Vortexes, the guild can make several belts. Prioritize the Vortex stockpile and finished belt order, not just the pattern recipient. |
