This is not a generic BiS list. It is an LC decision guide: who should receive the drop first, what gearing tradeoff it creates, and where an apparently "BiS" claim should lose to raid-level allocation.

## Priority Legend

| Label | Meaning |
|---|---|
| P0 | Protect this allocation. High raid impact, scarce, long-lived, or unlocks a major set bonus. |
| P1 | Strong first priority if the player will actually equip it soon. |
| P2 | Good later priority, completion piece, sidegrade, or fairness allocation. |
| P3 | Temp, PvP, niche, or "biggest upgrade" only. |
| Avoid | Do not award unless all real candidates are done or the guild has a special reason. |

## LC Executive Rules

1. Do not award solely by "appears on a BiS list." The video repeatedly prioritizes relative gain, scarcity, set unlock timing, and alternative availability.
2. Hero tokens are mage-led until every mage has 2p T5. The mage 2p Arcane Blast bonus is the strongest immediate Tier 5 set bonus in the video.
3. Champion tokens are rogue-led for a fast 4p Deathmantle plan. Rogue 4p is extremely powerful, but it should use tier helm/shoulders/gloves/legs plus an offset chest, not tier chest plus contested offset shoulders.
4. Hunters should generally not equip random T5 singles. BM hunters hold Beast Lord 4p until they can swap into 4p Rift Stalker together. Stack one hunter to 4p when ready.
5. Warlocks are low on Hero tokens because their real Phase 2 power is offpieces: Vestments of the Sea-Witch, Boots of the Shifting Nightmare or crafted Boots of Blasting, Wand of the Forgotten Star, Fathomstone, and caster accessories.
6. Vashj chest is a warlock item first. Do not roll it broadly to mages, boomkins, shadow priests, or "casters." Arcane mages should use T5 chest and later Gauntlets of the Sun King as the offset.
7. Belt of One-Hundred Deaths is not a hunter item. Expertise does not matter to hunters unless you are rewarding a fantasy melee-weave set.
8. Lightfathom Scepter is a healer drama item. The class delta is smaller than the player delta, so give it to the best or most reliable healer, then deliberately compensate other healers with cloak, rings, and tier.
9. Split physical rings by stat package: Ring of Lethality to agility users; Band of the Ranger-General to non-agility physical DPS.
10. For Nether Vortexes, solve blacksmith weapon upgrades first, then stockpile toward the patterns the raid will mass-use, especially Belt of Blasting.

## Specific Tradeoffs to Keep Straight

### Rogue: Tier Chest vs Offset Chest

Recommended rogue set:

| Slot | Plan |
|---|---|
| Helm | Deathmantle |
| Shoulders | Deathmantle |
| Gloves | Deathmantle |
| Legs | Deathmantle |
| Chest | Bloodsea Brigand's Vest |

Why this is preferred:
- It activates 4p without needing Kael'thas chest.
- It avoids putting Rogue into Shoulderpads of the Stranger, which should go Enhance > Ret > DPS Warrior.
- Bloodsea chest is harder for Rogue to replace than it is for Warrior.
- It lets Champion chest tokens go to Enhance/Resto/Ele/Holy instead of a Rogue who has a better LC path.

Non-recommended alternative:
- Tier chest plus offset shoulders. This consumes a Kael chest token and pushes Rogue into a contested Hydross shoulder item. Only use this if your raid's drop pattern or roster makes it unusually clean.

### Mage vs Warlock Chest

Arcane Mage has an excellent T5 chest path and can later use Gauntlets of the Sun King as the offset. Mage does not need the hit on Vestments of the Sea-Witch and already receives heavy token and weapon priority. Warlock needs the hit and has weak T5 options. Vashj chest should therefore be Warlock first.

### Hunter Token Timing

BM Hunter should hold Beast Lord 4p until 4p Rift Stalker is ready. This means:
- Do not award random non-token chest/legs to Hunter just because the item has hunter stats.
- Do not expect a Hunter to equip isolated T5 tokens immediately.
- Once Mage 2p obligations are handled, stack one Hunter to 4p rather than spreading pieces that sit in bags.

### Fury Warrior Payment Plan

Fury's new transcript review reinforces the existing LC pattern: Fury has real Phase 2 wants, but very few first claims.

- Keep Defender tokens low priority because the set bonus is weak and the token is shared with stronger set users.
- If the Fury is Hammersmith, `Dragonstrike` is the clean main-hand plan and Nether Vortexes should be funneled rather than split.
- If the Fury is not blacksmithing, or needs an offhand, `Talon of Azshara`, `Rod of the Sun King`, `Talon of the Phoenix`, and Orc `Netherbane` are all real paths, but each has a stronger first claimant.
- `Tsunami Talisman` is a legitimate non-DST Fury progression trinket, while `Bloodlust Brooch` stays useful on short/timed fights.

### Enhancement Weapon Logic

The new Enhancement sources support the priority on slow one-handers but caution against treating blacksmithing as automatic Tier 5 BiS.

- Two slow weapons with Windfury/Windfury are the high-scaling plan.
- Same-speed slow weapons are ideal if the player can manage sync/stagger; mismatched slow weapons remain strong.
- A fast offhand with Flametongue can work early, but should not beat a proper slow-weapon pair in Tier 5 loot routing.
- Blacksmithing is race- and phase-sensitive. Do not make Enhancement a default Nether Vortex sink unless the player has a specific crafted plan.

### Prot Paladin Tier Constraints

Prot Paladin wants some Champion tokens badly, but not all of them.

- T5 legs and gloves are strong, and helm can be strong if it fits the tank's uncrushable/threat route.
- T4 chest and Justicar/T4-style shoulders can remain correct because of block value and the old threat bonus.
- Champion shoulders and chest should stay low unless the Paladin can prove the full set math.
- `Darkener's Grasp`, `Girdle of the Invulnerable`, `Wristguards of Determination`, `Boots of the Resilient` / crafted boots, and sometimes `Fang of the Leviathan` are cleaner ways to pay Prot Paladin.

### Healer Mace Equity

Lightfathom Scepter is the strongest healer item in the phase. Since every healer wants it and the class delta is small:
- Give it to the best, most reliable, highest-impact healer.
- Use Sunshower Light Cloak, Coral Band of the Revived, Holy Priest tier, and other healer pieces to keep the rest of the core engaged.
- Resto Shaman may gain slightly more from the MP5, but not enough to override player quality.
- Priest `Earring of Soulful Meditation` is a narrow Priest trinket and should not be treated as broad healer compensation.
- Resto Druid gets real value from spirit through Tree of Life aura and Innervate, so spirit cloak/ring/offhand pieces are meaningful compensation when Defender tokens go elsewhere.
